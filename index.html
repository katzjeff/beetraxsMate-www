Managing your data

Data and information collected from your apiary and beehive is crucial in helping you make informed decisions. With BEETRaXs, you digitise and aggregate this data which in turns helps you improve your management expereince by reducing any inconvenice casued by lost or untraceable data.
Centralise your inspection reports, sales and production, easily track your inventory and most of all, simplify your identification process of your apiary and beehives.

DEMO

Track your metrics

Keeping track and documenting key metrics from helps you make informed decisions on the overall perfromance of your apiary and beehives.
Track your beehive temperature, humidity, overall weight and compare this with your production.
Remove any guess work from your decision making process.

DEMO

Remote Access

From the comfort of your home, remotely access your apiary and beehive and Keep track of activities. Be able to track your key metrics and make fast informed decision.
